# LLMs & Agents (for Recruitment / Jobboards)

## Why this topic matters
LLMs are reshaping recruitment-adjacent products: resume parsing and enrichment, job description normalization and quality scoring, conversational job search, candidate-job matching with reasoning, agentic flows where LLMs act on behalf of users, LLM-augmented messaging, and bias / fairness in LLM-driven hiring decisions (overlap with the trust & safety topic). For a jobboard the operational constraints are real — cost and latency matter, small/local models often win — so production-oriented papers and case studies are especially valuable.
