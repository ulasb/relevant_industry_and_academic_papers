# Online Marketplaces, Auctions & Bidding

## Why this topic matters
Many jobboards monetize through CPC or auction-based pricing: employers bid for visibility, the platform allocates impressions to balance revenue with relevance and user experience. The literature on auction design, bid optimization, budget pacing, value-based bidding, and incrementality measurement is large and constantly evolving. Two-sided marketplace dynamics (supply ↔ demand balance) and the interaction between auction outcomes and long-term value are particularly relevant.

## Relevance heuristics
- HIGH: production bidding systems, pacing under value uncertainty, multi-objective auctions (revenue + relevance), marketplace-level metrics & interference, learned auction mechanisms
- MEDIUM: incrementality / causal value estimation, mechanism design with practical assumptions
- LOW: pure theoretical auction theory without operationalization, narrow ad-tech tactics that don't generalize
